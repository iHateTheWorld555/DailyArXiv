We benchmark all four environmental knowledge levels: uniform current, ground-truth CFD, PatchGAN, and 2D3DGAN~SA across 19,800 independently generated trajectories spanning 550 distinct flow conditions. Full CFD wake knowledge reduces energy expenditure by 5.7-12.5% and high-velocity wake-core encounters by up to 77.8% relative to uniform-current planning, with both benefits scaling with operating severity. The cGAN surrogates recover approximately 45-60% of the CFD energy benefit and high-velocity cell avoidance benefit while operating at inference speeds compatible with edge device use.
